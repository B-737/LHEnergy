Building Registration

The first step to obtain EPCs for your buildings is to register your buildings on National Building Energy Performance Register here by owners or accounting officers.

For ease, you can contact us, we can register your buildings on your request.

Assessment

We will perform an Energy Performance Assessment (EPA) specific to your property.

The documents provided by you may consist of: Building plans, Energy bills, Light systems, Air conditioning systems, Solar power.

verified

Certification

The assessment will be verified by Department of Mineral Resources & Energy (DMRE) after which we will issue the certificate for your building.

You will then receive your EPC
